Panda

The giant panda's black and white coat and prominent black eye patches have made it one of the best known species, although it is among the shyest and rarest animals in the world.

Area: China
Habitat: Forest
Food: Bamboo, grasses, bulbs, some insects and fruit
Size: 5 feet long

The giant panda is a solitary animal, spending about two-thirds of its day feeding and the remainder resting.About 2,000 pandas are found in the wild. The giant panda is actually a bear. The life span of giant pandas in the wild is approximately 20 years.
